## Queue Composition

Your daily queue is algorithmically built using multiple principles:

### Priority 1: Due Reviews (70%) â€” *Spaced Repetition*
Items where `nextReviewAt â‰¤ today`, sorted by how overdue they are.
These are concepts scheduled for review at the optimal time for retention.

### Priority 2: New Items (30%) â€” *Elaboration*
Next IPs from your current lesson, capped to prevent overload.
New items enter through Learn Mode where they're connected to prior knowledge.

### Interleaving â€” *Interleaving*
Items are mixed together (not all reviews then all new):
```
Review (Lesson 1) â†’ New (Lesson 2) â†’ Review (Lesson 3) â†’ Review (Lesson 1) â†’ ...
```

This isn't randomâ€”**interleaving forces you to discriminate between concepts** from different lessons, improving your ability to recognize which approach applies in a given situation.
