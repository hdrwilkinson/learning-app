## Research Areas

- SM-2 algorithm (SuperMemo)
- Modern variations (FSRS, Anki)

### AI in Education
How LLMs can support learning:
- Tutoring systems research
- Intelligent question generation
- Adaptive learning systems

### Learning Platform Design
What works in digital learning:
- Microlearning effectiveness
- Gamification trade-offs
- Mobile learning patterns
